# Sentria Samadhan: Project Interview

### 1. The Core Identity

- **Official Project Title:**
  AI-Based Citizen Grievance Classification System (Sentria Samadhan)

- **One-Sentence Summary (Elevator Pitch):**
  An AI-powered citizen grievance management platform that enables citizens to report issues, automatically classifies complaints using artificial intelligence, and helps government authorities track, manage, and resolve them through an intelligent dashboard.

---

### 2. The Context

- **Problem Statement:**
  Public grievance systems often suffer from slow complaint processing, manual categorization, poor tracking, and inefficient communication between citizens and authorities. The challenge was to design an intelligent platform that streamlines complaint management while improving transparency and response time.

- **Hackathon Details:**
  Built for **BGI Hackathon 2026 – Vision 2047 | Viksit Bharat** under the theme **AI, Data Science & Intelligent Automation**.

**Achievement**

- 🏆 National Winner
- 💰 ₹25,000 Prize
- Competed against 600+ teams and over 2800 participants nationwide.

---

### 3. The Execution

- **The Solution:**
  Sentria Samadhan provides a unified platform where citizens can report civic issues while administrators monitor, classify, and manage complaints through dedicated dashboards. Artificial Intelligence assists in categorizing grievances, making the complaint management process faster and more efficient.

- **Key Features:**
- AI-powered grievance classification
- Citizen complaint submission portal
- Interactive complaint map
- Administrative dashboard
- Analytics and reporting dashboard
- Region and authority management
- Complaint tracking workflow
